Biography

I’m a first year PhD student at the University of Florida in the Florida Institute for Cybersecurity Research (FICS) Lab, advised by Dr. Daniela Oliveira. I love to work at the nexus of Security and Artificial Intelligence. My interests include user privacy (phishing, disinformation, online abuse), identity and access management, and vulnerability detection and analysis.
